Welcome to the SIM activation page.

    To activate your SIM card, please have the following information ready:



    -Your SIM card number

    -Your personal details, including your full name, date of birth, and address.

    -Your mobile number (if you have a number you want to transfer to our network)


      Once you have this information, follow these steps:


      1) Insert your SIM card into your phone and turn it on.

    2) Wait for the activation message to appear on your screen.

    3) Follow the instructions on the screen to complete the activation process.


    If you're transferring your existing number to our network, please allow up to 24 hours for the transfer to complete.


    If you need any help with the activation process, please contact our customer support team. We're available 24/7 to help you with any questions or issues you may have.


    Thank you for choosing our mobile service. We hope you enjoy your new SIM card and the benefits of our network.
