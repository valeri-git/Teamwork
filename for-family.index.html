Family plan:

Our Family Plan is designed for families who want to stay connected while saving money.


With this plan, families can enjoy 30GB of high-speed data, which is perfect for streaming movies, browsing the web, and keeping up with social media.


The plan includes unlimited talk and text within the US, so family members can stay in touch without worrying about additional charges.


Plus, with discounted international calling rates, family members can stay connected with their loved ones abroad without breaking the bank.


This plan allows for up to 4 lines, so every family member can have their own phone and data allowance.
